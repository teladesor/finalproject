About Us

Stanford is here to help enhance your organization's image and increase its visibility with membership and award certificates. We offer design and personalization services for certificates and awards with a minimum setup quantity of 25 pieces. Framing and insertion services are available, and we can bulk ship to your location or drop ship directly to your customers.

Stanford Ceritificate & Diploma Services is owned and operated by CPAdirect Marketing, Inc.

Our Commitment and Guarantee

Your 100% satisfaction is our objective, and guarantee. Whether you are purchasing through one of our partners, or directly from us, we want to assure your experience is a positive one and that you LOVE the certificate or frame we create for you. We are committed to providing you with high-quality documents that will preserve and professionally display your most important milestones and achievements. Each product is custom made to order, and we manufacture all of our certificates and frames here in the United States. We maintain stringent standards in product quality and inspection, to deliver a product that will exceed your expectations.

Return Policy

We create the finest quality products and want you to be 100% pleased with your purchase. It is not often you see companies making promises these days, but we can promise you this... You will love your purchase from us! Each certificate is individually crafted and carefully inspected to ensure it meets our high-quality standards. If you are not satisfied for any reason, please let us know. We will work with you to make it right! If you have a problem with your order, please contact a member of our Customer Service.

Shipping Information

All orders are custom-made at our facility, so please allow 5-7 business days for the production of your order, plus FedEx Ground transit time. As a general rule, you can expect to have your order “in-hands” in approximately 10 business days. If you need your order sooner or for a specific event or date, please contact us for accelerated production and shipping options during the checkout process.
